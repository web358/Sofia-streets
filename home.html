Life in Sofia

Despite the "cultivation" and the huge improvement in the center of the City, which may seem attractive as a tourist site, the city still hasn't lost its real face and as any capital in the world it has many urban sites to offer...
What really can be interesting for Bulgarians themseleves is that if you type "sofia streets" in the search bar, under the heading "People also ask " you will find the question "Where is the red light district in Sofia?" and (maybe not surprisingly) an answer (what's more a correct one) immediately appears: "The red light district near Pliska Hotel and park areas near Lion's Bridge, or Lvov Most, also are considered unsafe, and occasionally muggings do occur."
